I am a systems researcher and research manager of the [Systems Research Group](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/systems-research-group-asia/) (SRG) at [Microsoft Research Asia](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-asia/) (MSR Asia).

My research passion lies in computer systems. Recently, I have focused on systems for Artificial Intelligence (AI): AI compilers and kernels such as TileLang, vector stores and databases such as VBase, wafer-scale LLM inference systems such as WaferLLM, and reasoning engines that combine agentic and neuro-symbolic approaches such as PolyaMath and rStar.

I am interested in building principled, high-performance systems that make intelligent applications more efficient, reliable, and broadly useful.
